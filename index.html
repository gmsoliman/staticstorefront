Plant of the Day!

Peace Lily

Did you know that peace lilies give off their oxygen at night? They're also very good at removing chemicals from the air such as Benzene and Trichloroethylene. Put one of these in your bedroom and you can breathe easy knowing you'll have a more "peaceful" sleep.
